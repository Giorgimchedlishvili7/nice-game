⚠ Screen Time Awareness

Doctors and child development experts warn that spending too much time playing digital games can negatively affect young people. Overuse may cause problems such as reduced sleep, poor school performance, addictive behaviors, and spending less time socializing or exercising. Staying active and keeping life balanced is important.

Excessive gaming can also:

  • Lead to eye strain and headaches from screen overuse.
  • Increase feelings of anxiety or irritability when not playing.
  • Cause poor posture and physical discomfort from sitting long periods.
  • Make real-life responsibilities feel boring or unimportant.
  • Encourage aggressive behavior if violent games are overused.
  • Impact attention span, making schoolwork or reading harder.

Enjoy games in moderation and balance screen time with outdoor activities, hobbies, and social time.
