Healthy Screen Time

⚠ Screen Time Awareness

Experts warn that spending too much time playing digital games can negatively affect cognitive development. Overuse may cause reduced sleep, poor performance, and decreased social interaction.

  • Reduced attention spans and focus
  • Increased anxiety or irritability
  • Physical discomfort and poor posture
  • Distorted perception of real-world priorities
  • Aggressive behavioral shifts
  • Significant impact on sleep hygiene

Balance is the key to digital health. Pair your gaming with physical activity and real-world social connection.
